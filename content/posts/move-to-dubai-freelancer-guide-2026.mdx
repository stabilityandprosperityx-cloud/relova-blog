## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the first step before I commit financially?**

Start by validating visa eligibility, timeline, and monthly cash flow. Do not sign long leases or transfer deposits until your legal path is clear.

**How much emergency buffer should I keep for the move?**

Keep at least 15 to 25 percent above your planned setup and monthly budget. This covers document issues, rebookings, and exchange-rate swings.

**Should I rent before arrival or after I land?**

For most movers, a short-term stay first is safer. It lets you inspect neighborhoods, verify internet quality, and avoid overpriced listings.

**Can I handle the process myself without a lawyer?**

Many straightforward cases are DIY-friendly, but legal review is smart for edge cases, multiple passports, or complex income structures.

**How long until relocation feels stable?**

Most people feel stable around the 60 to 90 day mark once banking, health coverage, registrations, and housing are fully operational.
