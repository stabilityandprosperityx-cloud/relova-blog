**Should I sign a long lease immediately for move to Saudi Arabia 2026?**

Usually no. Start medium-term and switch after validating commute, noise, and landlord quality.

**Do I need local language skills for move to Saudi Arabia 2026?**

Entry may be possible in English, but daily life and bureaucracy improve quickly with basic local language.

**When should I hire legal or tax help for move to Saudi Arabia 2026?**

Hire early if you have dependants, cross-border income, prior refusals, or property purchases.
