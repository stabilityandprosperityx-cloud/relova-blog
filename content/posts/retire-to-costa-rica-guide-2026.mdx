Keep fifteen to twenty-five percent above modeled spend for at least ninety days after arrival.

**Should I sign a long lease immediately for retire to Costa Rica 2026?**

Usually no. Start medium-term and switch after validating commute, noise, and landlord quality.

**Do I need local language skills for retire to Costa Rica 2026?**

Entry may be possible in English, but daily life and bureaucracy improve quickly with basic local language.

**When should I hire legal or tax help for retire to Costa Rica 2026?**

Hire early if you have dependants, cross-border income, prior refusals, or property purchases.
