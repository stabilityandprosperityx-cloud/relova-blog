## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the safest first step for visa fees comparison 2026?**

Start with route fit, not destination hype. Verify legal eligibility, income thresholds, and processing timelines first, then build your checklist around official requirements and appointment lead times.

**How much money should I keep as a relocation buffer?**

Budget both setup and recurring costs. A practical buffer is 15-25% above your base model to absorb delays, duplicate filings, and exchange-rate shifts during onboarding.

**Should I sign a long lease before arrival?**

Use medium-term housing for arrival unless terms are flexible. It gives you time to validate neighborhood fit, transport realities, and landlord reliability before long commitments.

**Do I need local language fluency before moving?**

Language is not always mandatory for approval, but it strongly improves daily life: healthcare access, admin communication, social integration, and job opportunities over time.

**When should I hire legal or tax support?**

Complex cases benefit from specialist review. Mixed income, dependants, prior refusals, or cross-border tax exposure usually justify expert support before filing.
